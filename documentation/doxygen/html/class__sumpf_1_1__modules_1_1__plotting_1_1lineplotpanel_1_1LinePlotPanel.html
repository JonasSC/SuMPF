◆ SetCursors()

  cursors

Draws cursors at the given positions at the x-Axis.

The number of cursors will be the number of given lines. In this case, "cursors" does not mean the mouse cursor, but a vertical line, that indicates a position on the x-Axis. If the cursors are hidden, it might be useful to make them visible with the ShowCursors method.

Parameters
cursorsa list of x-Axis values, where cursors shall be shown
