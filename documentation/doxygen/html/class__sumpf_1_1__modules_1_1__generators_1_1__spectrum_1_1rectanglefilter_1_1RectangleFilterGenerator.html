Detailed Description

Generates a Spectrum whose samples outside the given frequency band are zero and one inside the frequency band.

It is also possible to invert the spectrum, so that the samples outside the frequency band are one and the inside samples are zero.

Specifies, if the filter function shall be inverted.

If the inversion is set to False, all samples outside the frequency band are zero, while the samples inside the frequency band are one. If it is True, the samples outside the frequency band are one, while the inside samples are zero.
