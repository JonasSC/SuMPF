Detailed Description

A class whose instances provide compatible format information for the two ChannelData subclasses "Signal" and "Spectrum".

This helps to generate Signals whose Fourier transformation is compatible in length and resolution to a generated Spectrum. Of course the transformed Spectrum will also be compatible to the generated Signal. Compatibility means that the length and the sampling rate or resolution are equal. This is often required. For example for merging the data sets or for algebraic operations.
